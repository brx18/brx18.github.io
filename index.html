I am currently an AI Scientist at GE Healthcare. Prior to this, I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the University of Pittsburgh and completed my Honours B.S. in Electrical Engineering at the University of Science and Technology of China.

My current work focuses on Large Language Model (LLM) research and applications, particularly in:

  • Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and In-Context Learning
  • Transfer Learning for Healthcare
  • Model Compression and Optimization

I’m open to collaboration opportunities—feel free to reach out!
